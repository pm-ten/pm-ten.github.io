An innovative project which takes care of the environment in line with the principles of the circular economy. 

The scientific contribution of PM_TEN to the Fishing for Litter campaigns created by Findus with the collaboration of Ogyre (<a href="https://www.findus.it/sostenibilita/pesce/oceani/fishing-for-litter" target="_blank">Fishing For Litter, for a cleaner sea - Findus</a>) and all fishermen. 

We start from Santa Margherita Ligure with the classification of accidentally caught waste and the delineation of transformation and reuse scenarios.
