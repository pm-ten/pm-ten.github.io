The Kick-off Meeting of the PROMPT project was held in Palazzo S.Giorgio, funded under the DG-ECHO Union Civil Protection Mechanism program of the European Community to develop a decision support system and intervene in case of spill accidents in sea. 

Based on the integration of water and air dispersion simulations, the project is coordinated by the University of Genoa and sees the participation of PM_TEN together with IH Cantabria, OEPT (Port of Tripoli), University of Jordan, WASDI and European Research Institute
