---

Presented at Rolli Days the IN-HERITAGE project. 

In collaboration with Artys and HOPU, the winning project of the challenge within the SME4SMARTCITIES initiative (ENI CBC Med Programme).

A platform dedicated to the evaluation of the effects of air pollution on the conservation of cultural heritage, with the use of innovative monitoring systems (smart-sensors) and the inclusion of emerging pollutants (Black Carbon measured thanks to the optical method integrated in the GianoBC1).
