Last week we had the pleasure of taking part in the **XI National Conference on Atmospheric Particulate Matter, PM2024**, organised by the IAS - the Italian Aerosol Society, in collaboration with Arpa Piemonte and the University of Turin - an important opportunity for discussion and updating on the various topics related to atmospheric particulate matter. 
 
There were two contributions:  
 
- Silvia Giulia Danelli's Talk entitled ‘Evaluation of standard procedures for the collection and measurement of carbonaceous aerosol in the workplace: The CALIPSO project’, financed by the Liguria 2021/2027 ERDF R.P. programme.
  
- The Poster entitled ‘An integrated sensor-model approach for early-warning and decision-support systems’ presented by Matilde De Camillis and Silvia Perasso on our new monitoring platform DigitalPlant.
