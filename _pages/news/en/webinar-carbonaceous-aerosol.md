On September 18, we held our first in-depth webinar, and we are very happy for the large participation recorded!

This event, which involved numerous colleagues and industry professionals, provided an opportunity to deepen our understanding of a class of compounds that deserve significant attention. Carbonaceous aerosols, especially prevalent in urban settings and largely due to anthropogenic combustion processes, play a crucial role both in terms of climate and human health.

The study of carbonaceous aerosols has always been a key theme of our corporate identity, and the idea for this informative webinar arose from our desire to share this knowledge in response to the growing attention being paid to these pollutants in both air quality and occupational hygiene contexts.

**Topics Covered:**

- **Importance and impact of carbonaceous aerosols**

- **Sampling methods in ambient air and workplaces**

- **The most used analytical techniques and their specific complexities**

- **Current regulations: discussion on existing standards and market needs**
