### CLIMATOLOGICAL ANALYSIS AND SIMULATION OF PAST EVENTS
Climatological studies on a multi-decade basis exploioting mesoscale numerical models, forced by planetary-scale models (reanalysis), and reconstruction of past meteorological events in relation to different types of atmospheric observables, such as wind, precipitation, temperature, solar radiation, etc. 

### DELIVERY ON AN OPERATIONAL BASIS OF METEOROLOGICAL FIELDS
Delivery of meteorological fields (pressure, wind, temperature, humidity, precipitation, solar radiation, etc.) on a (at least) daily basis for different forecast intervals on areas selected by the customer, at continental and regional scale.

### VERY HIGH-RESOLUTION METEOROLOGICAL FIELDS SIMULATIONS
Production of meteorological forecasting and/or climatological fields with very high spatial detail (up to tens of metres) by coupling mesoscale weather models with both diagnostic (e.g. mass-consistent) and prognostic (e.g. Large-Eddy Simulation) codes.
- **FOR STUDYING DISPERSION OF POLLUTANTS INTO THE ATMOSPHERE**
- **FOR STUDYING FOREST FIRE PROPAGATION**

### PRODUCTION OF OCEANOGRAPHIC INTEREST FIELDS
Simulations of ocean currents and wave dynamics at the basin scales forced by mesoscale and/or high-resolution meteorological models, both for forecast needs and for climatological studies.
