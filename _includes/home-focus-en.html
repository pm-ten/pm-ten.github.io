PM_TEN srl is an innovative SME that connects the world of university research to the entrepreneurial market, positioning itself as a driving force for further progress in methodologies developed in the academic sphere.
PM_TEN srl was born as a spin-off of the Environmental Physics Laboratory of the University of Genoa and is part of a network of laboratories and university spin-offs with diversified and complementary competences.
PM_TEN srl is also part of the TICASS (Tecnologie Innovative per il Controllo Ambientale e lo Sviluppo Sostenibile) consortium, managing body of the Regional Technological Innovation Pole "Energy-Environment" for the Liguria Region.
The company's main activities concern:

  • Determination of different types of pollutants (dust, radioactive isotopes, noise, non- ionising radiation) and their sources in environmental matrices
  • Compositional analysis of atmospheric particulate matter (environment and workplace)
  • Characterisation of air pollution sources
  • Simulation using mathematical models of pollutant dispersion
  • Support for funded projects and process management
  • Compositional analysis of materials (including non-destructive techniques)
  • Meteorological and marine forecasting
  • Study of wind potential
