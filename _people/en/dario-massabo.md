title: Dario Massabò
---

Researcher at the University of Genoa.

Master degree and PhD in Science and Engineering of Materials. 

Skills and expertise: 
- Field measures for air quality control. 
- Measurement techniques and analysis for the characterization of atmospheric pollution and its sources, in particular Energy Dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF).
- Optical and thermo-optical techniques for determining the carbonaceous fraction (organic and elemental carbon) in atmospheric particulate matter.
- Growth and characterization of thin films, nanostructuring of surfaces by ion beams, preparation and management of ultra high vacuum (UHV) systems.
- Atomic force microscopy (AFM), Mass spectrometry (SIMS), X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), Optical spectrometry.
